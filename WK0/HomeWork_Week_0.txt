Pseudo Code for Making a Cup of Tea for 1 Person.
-------------------------------------------------
Inputs: Milk,tea bag,sugar,cup,and kettle.
Output: Tea
Process Steps/Algorithm:  
Start
	Organise everything together;
	Empty kettle;
	Plug in kettle;
	Put tea bag in the cup;
	Pour water up to the required level into kettle;
	Wait for the water to boil;
	Pour the hot water from kettle to the cup;
	Wait for 2 minutes;
	Remove tea bag with spoon/fork from the cup;
	Add milk as desired;
	If sugar is required:
		add sugar;
	else
		do nothing;
	
	Serve;
Stop.
